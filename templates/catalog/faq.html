When purchasing a serialized firearm (rifle, handgun, shotgun, or stripped lower receiver), federal law requires us to ship the item to a licensed Federal Firearms Licensee (FFL) in your state. During checkout, simply select your preferred local gun shop or shooting range from our verified FFL network. Once your order arrives at their facility, they will conduct your background check and complete the transfer.

Because Glocks And Armor is a personal armory store with 100% in-house inventory (no marketplace third-party drop-shippers), all non-serialized parts, optics, magazines, reloading supplies, and gunsmithing tools ship directly to your front door within 24 hours of order placement.

We utilize automated ATF e-Form 3 transfers to dispatch NFA suppressors to your local Class 3 SOT dealer within 2 to 5 business days. Once received by your dealer, they will assist you with filing your ATF e-Form 4 (Trust or Individual) for rapid digital approval.

We stand behind every piece of ordnance and gear leaving our hub. We offer a 100% Money-Back Guarantee on all unmodified gun parts, optics, and accessories within 30 days of receipt.

AR-15 / AR-10 TORQUE SPECIFICATION CHART

Always use a quality torque wrench and proper grease/threadlocker when assembling upper and lower receivers.

COMPONENT / PART RECOMMENDED TORQUE SPEC THREAD COMPOUND / NOTES
Barrel Nut (Standard Mil-Spec) 30 - 80 ft-lbs AeroShell 33MS / MS28654 Grease. Align gas tube hole.
Castle Nut (Receiver Extension) 38 - 42 ft-lbs Stake end plate into castle nut notches in 2 places.
Muzzle Device / Flash Hider 15 - 20 ft-lbs Use crush washer or peel washer. Do not overtorque suppressors.
Gas Key Screws (Carrier) 50 - 58 in-lbs Permatex High-Temp Threadlocker & properly stake bolt carrier.
